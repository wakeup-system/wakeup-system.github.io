WakeUp System

Wards of the Future

The Future of Healthcare

Developed in partnership with University College London (UCL), Great Ormond Street Hospital (GOSH) and Intel, the WakeUp System provides reassurance and support to patients with disabilities in long-term care or the elderly. One of the system's main objectives is to enable them to autonomously control their environment, and it is designed for use in both hospital and home settings. A key part of the project is the integration of Matter, a new application layer connectivity protocol for smart home devices. Moreover, the project encompasses a research study in Intel's new OpenVINO project.
